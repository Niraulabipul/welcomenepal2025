Discover the Sacred Temples of Nepal

Explore the rich cultural and spiritual heritage of Nepal

Pashupatinath Temple

Pashupatinath, located in Kathmandu, is one of the holiest Hindu temples dedicated to Lord Shiva. It is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and a center of spirituality and cultural significance.

Swayambhunath Stupa

Also known as the "Monkey Temple," Swayambhunath is a stunning Buddhist stupa situated atop a hill. The panoramic views of Kathmandu and the serene atmosphere make it a must-visit.

Boudhanath Stupa

Boudhanath is one of the largest stupas in the world and a significant pilgrimage site for Buddhists. Located in Kathmandu, it is surrounded by vibrant Tibetan culture.

Muktinath Temple

Muktinath, located in Mustang, is a sacred site for both Hindus and Buddhists. The temple is known for its natural eternal flame and 108 water spouts, symbolizing purification.
