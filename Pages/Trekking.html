Trekking and Hiking in Nepal

Discover the best trekking routes and hiking trails Nepal has to offer

Annapurna Circuit

The Annapurna Circuit is a legendary trekking route offering breathtaking views of the Annapurna range. With diverse landscapes, it passes through traditional villages, lush forests, and high mountain passes like the Thorong La at 5,416 meters.

Everest Base Camp Trek

Embark on the iconic trek to Everest Base Camp, a bucket-list adventure for many. Experience Sherpa culture, stunning glaciers, and the mighty peaks of the Everest region on this unforgettable journey.

Langtang Valley Trek

Known as the "Valley of Glaciers," the Langtang Valley Trek offers panoramic views of snow-capped mountains, serene forests, and local Tamang culture. It's a relatively short yet enriching trekking experience.

Manaslu Circuit

The Manaslu Circuit Trek offers a remote and adventurous trail encircling the majestic Manaslu peak. It's an off-the-beaten-path experience filled with dramatic landscapes and a unique cultural immersion.

Ghorepani Poon Hill Trek

A short and scenic trek, the Poon Hill Trek is ideal for beginners. Witness sunrise over the Annapurna and Dhaulagiri ranges, explore lush rhododendron forests, and visit vibrant local villages.

Mardi Himal Trek

The Mardi Himal Trek is a hidden gem offering stunning views of Mardi Himal and Machhapuchhre. This trek takes you through tranquil forests, high ridges, and spectacular mountain vistas.

Why Trek in Nepal?

Nepal is a trekker's paradise, offering a blend of natural beauty, cultural richness, and unmatched adventure. Whether you're seeking high-altitude challenges or easy hiking trails, Nepal's diverse trekking routes cater to every level of trekker.
